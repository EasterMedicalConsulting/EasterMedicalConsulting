## About Me

For 18 years, I've practiced pediatrics. For the last 10, I've also been part of building the data infrastructure that helps health systems care for populations — not just individual patients. And now I'm building the apps I wish existed for my patients.

I'm dual board-certified in **Pediatrics** and **Clinical Informatics**, a Fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics (FAAP) and the American Medical Informatics Association (FAMIA), and I hold an MBA/MHA. This combination has allowed me to work at the intersection of clinical insight, data science, and organizational leadership.

I continue to see patients — outpatient pediatrics, urgent care, and inpatient hospitalist. For me, staying clinically active keeps the work grounded. Every workflow I evaluate, every data problem I try to solve, every tool I build — the patient in front of me last week is never far from my thinking.
